Emily Mackevicius

    Emily Mackevicius is a co-founder and director of Basis Research Institute, a member of the Simons Society of Fellows, and a postdoc in the Aronov lab and the Center for Theoretical Neuroscience at Columbiaâ€™s Zuckerman Institute. She is interested in how intelligent behaviors emerge, especially in distributed and recurrent systems. Her research uncovers how complex cognitive behaviors are generated by networks of neurons through local learning rules. Her theoretical work is strongly grounded in experimental practice, currently neural recordings in food-caching birds with extreme memory abilities.
